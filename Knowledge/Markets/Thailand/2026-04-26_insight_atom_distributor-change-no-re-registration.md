claim: >
  Foreign brands do not need full product re-registration when changing Thai distributors as registration is tied to the product (held by manufacturer or original registrant), but new distributor must update importer details via FDA notification and obtain new import licence per shipment.
why_it_matters: >
  This reduces switching costs and barriers for Nordic brands to change Thai distribution partners, providing flexibility in go-to-market strategy without regulatory reset. However, per-shipment import licence requirements remain with each distributor change.
next_action: >
  For N→T clients considering distributor changes, structure agreements to ensure smooth FDA notification process for importer detail updates and confirm new distributor's capability to handle per-shipment import licence requirements.
