claim: >
  Imported bottled drinking water in sealed containers is classified as Category 2 food under Thai Food Act B.E. 2522 (1979) Section 15, requiring FDA product registration (food serial number) before first importation, and the importer must be a Thai-registered entity with a place of business.
why_it_matters: >
  This classification determines the regulatory pathway and timeline for Nordic water brands entering Thailand. Category 2 status means quality/standard requirements but avoids more complex controlled food procedures, making market entry more straightforward for compliant products.
next_action: >
  For any N→T water client, confirm Thai entity registration status first, then initiate FDA product registration process with food serial number application as the critical first step before any importation can begin.
