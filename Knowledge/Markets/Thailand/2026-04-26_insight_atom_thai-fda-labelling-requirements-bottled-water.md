  Thai FDA requires full Thai-language labels for bottled water including ingredients, allergens, and mandatory identifiers, with non-compliance halting customs release. Digital permit linkage (LPI token) to invoice required per shipment, while QR code labelling remains optional as of April 2026 despite September 2025 draft proposals.

  Label compliance is a hard stop at customs, making Thai-language labelling a critical path item for Nordic brands. The per-shipment LPI token requirement creates ongoing operational complexity beyond initial registration.

  For N→T beverage clients, budget for Thai label design and printing as part of launch costs, and establish LPI token generation process with logistics partner before first shipment to avoid customs delays.
