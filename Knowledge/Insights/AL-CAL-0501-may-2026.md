# May 2026 Editorial Calendar — Andaman Life

## Monthly theme: Low-season advantage

**Editorial rationale:** May marks the onset of Phuket's rainy season — tourist crowds thin and the island returns to its resident rhythm. This is the right time to publish content that serves the actual living experience rather than holiday planning. May also coincides with international school year-end and the mid-year point in expat planning cycles, creating natural hooks for education, property, visa, and financial review content.

---

## Research intelligence (Perplexity, April 2026)

**Visa and immigration signals for AL-BRIEF-0502:**
- LTR Visa: unchanged in 2026. 10-year renewable, 50,000 Baht application fee. Income-exempt on foreign-sourced earnings. Annual reporting replaces 90-day check-ins. Wealthy Pensioner pathway: age 50+, USD 80,000 annual passive income or USD 40,000+ with USD 250,000 invested in Thailand.
- Proposed visa-exempt reduction (60 days → 30 days): NOT yet enacted as of April 2026. 60-day period remains in force.
- Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC): operational since May 1, 2025 — replaces paper TM.6.
- e-Visa: expanded to all 94 Thai embassies globally from January 1, 2025.
- Non-Immigrant retirement visa conditions: under government review; no changes finalised.
