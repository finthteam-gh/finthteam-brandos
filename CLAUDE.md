## What HEARTBAKER is

HEARTBAKER is a premium Thai bakery brand with 15 years of history. Founded and run by Jere's sister, who leads production. Thailand only. Target audience: upper middle to high-end retail customers.

HEARTBAKER is a brand built on quality, craft, and personal connection — not mass production. Distribution is relationship-led: word of mouth, loyal returning customers, Facebook Messenger, and LINE Official Account.

---

## Current product range

**Hero product — Cupcake line**
16 flavours. The brand's signature product since founding, approximately 15 years ago. Still the commercial core of the business.

**Catering service**
Reduced activity but still accepted for events. Not actively marketed.

**New launch — The Aperitif Collection**
Butter Bread Stick targeting wine and premium beverage enjoyers.
Official tagline: "Made for wine and good company."
Packaging: Size S (85g), Size M (135g), Size L (250g).
Pricing: under finalisation.
Positioning: a product people are proud to give as a gift, bring to a gathering, or share at a lounge, bar, wine-tasting, or social occasion.
Target customer: wine enthusiasts, premium beverage consumers, lounge and bar regulars, wine-tasting society members.
Distribution: to be defined — likely gifting, direct order, premium retail, and hospitality venue placement.

---

## Production model

Production led by Jere's sister. Small operation — runs lean with temporary staff brought in for high-volume periods. Capacity is a real constraint and must be factored into all planning.

No permanent staff currently. Scale-up path: hiring additional hands as demand grows.

---

## Current channels

- Word of mouth (primary acquisition)
- Returning loyal customers
- Facebook Messenger (order communication)
- LINE Official Account (primary customer communication channel)
- Facebook and Instagram (brand presence — not yet primary sales channels)

No physical retail store. No e-commerce website currently active.

---

## Brand identity

- Name always written: HEARTBAKER (all caps)
- Established brand identity and logo in place
- Active Instagram and Facebook accounts
- LINE Official Account for customer communication
- Brand has emotional depth — 15 years, family-run, genuine craft
- Tone: warm, confident, unpretentious, quality-led

## Production constraint rule

Always account for production capacity in any plan. HEARTBAKER cannot scale faster than the production team can realistically handle. Any campaign or launch plan must include a capacity check and a realistic volume ceiling.
