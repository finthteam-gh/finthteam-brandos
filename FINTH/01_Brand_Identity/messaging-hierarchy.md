# Messaging Hierarchy — FINTH

## Core Message (one sentence)
FINTH is the boutique studio that helps quality-driven and conscious brands move between the Nordic world and Southeast Asia without losing their essence.

## Supporting Messages

**For Profile A (Nordic executive entering Thailand):**
You do not need a market report. You need someone who has already built relationships in Bangkok and Phuket, understands the regulatory reality, and can shortcut the two years it would otherwise cost you.

**For Profile B (Thai business leader entering the Nordics):**
The Nordic market rewards authenticity and penalises noise. FINTH opens the right doors in Helsinki and helps you position your brand in a way that earns respect — not curiosity.

**On the corridor:**
The Nordic–SEA corridor is moving faster than most companies realise. The EU–Thailand FTA, the clean energy agenda, and the wellness economy are creating real commercial openings. FINTH helps you act before the window closes.

## Proof Points

- Acting as IOR and TFDA licence holder for JÄÄDE Water — Finland's first sparkling water brand entering Thailand
- CEO based between Helsinki, Bangkok, and Phuket — not a remote market watcher
- Structured CFO and CMO research programmes across 45+ modules covering the corridor
- Silver Economy research tracking Nordic 55+ demographic as emerging Thailand-bound audience

## What FINTH Never Says

- "Asia is a huge opportunity" — too vague, not our voice
- "We are your one-stop solution" — not boutique language
- "World-class" or "best-in-class" — empty
- Any claim that requires expertise we do not have
