# Brand Values — FINTH

## Core Values

**Intellectual precision**
FINTH thinks before it speaks. Every claim is grounded. Every recommendation is calibrated to the specific client, market, and moment. No generic advice.

**Cultural authenticity**
Jere lives both worlds. FINTH does not perform cultural competence — it is built from it. Finnish precision and Thai adaptability are not marketing claims, they are lived operating principles.

**Quality over volume**
FINTH is small and selective by design. It takes on fewer clients and does the work properly. This is the only way to protect the reputation that makes the corridor model work.

**Realism as a service**
FINTH tells clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Showing both upside and risk is not pessimism — it is the only honest way to help a brand build presence that lasts.

**Work that lasts**
Every engagement is built to still make sense in five years. FINTH does not chase short-term wins that compromise long-term positioning.

---

## What FINTH Is Not

- Not a large agency with generalist staff
- Not a market research firm producing reports
- Not a passive intermediary or introducer
- Not a temporary project team — FINTH builds ongoing relationships
- Not a brand that competes on price

---

## FINTH's Competitive Position

FINTH sits in a position no large consultancy or generic agency can occupy: a founder-led boutique with genuine dual-country presence, cultural fluency in both markets, and a track record of operational execution (not just strategy).

The corridor between Finland and Southeast Asia is real and underserved. FINTH's edge is that Jere has lived it.
