# Audience Personas — FINTH

## Profile A — Nordic Executive

**Who:** Finnish or Nordic founder, commercial director, or market entry manager
**Age:** 35–55, budget authority, hands-on decision maker
**Sectors:** Health, medtech, wellness, cleantech, SaaS, food tech, design
**Situation:** Considering or actively pursuing Thailand/SEA — has read a Business Finland report but hasn't moved

**Key pains:**
- Fears wasting a year on the wrong partner or entry model
- Has no real local contacts who can move a deal forward
- Worried about cultural blind spots and brand translation failure
- Needs to show board progress on internationalisation

**What they want:** One trusted boutique studio that knows both sides and can shortcut the learning curve

**Conversion trigger:** 3–5 FINTH posts that name their exact problem → direct LinkedIn DM to CEO

**Content that works:** P2 (market entry realities), P4 (partnerships), P3 (brand translation)

**Direction tag:** N→T

**Hashtags:** #Nordicbusiness #ThailandBusiness #marketentry #Nordichealth #cleantech

## Profile B — Thai Business Leader

**Who:** Thai founder, MD, or commercial head — wellness clinics, spa chains, food brands, design businesses, wellness tourism operators, industrial exporters
**Age:** 38–60, well-connected in Thailand, limited European network
**Situation:** Successful Thai business, starting to consider Europe — premium positioning or CBAM regulatory pressure

**Key pains:**
- No Nordic network, no understanding of European buyer psychology
- Unsure how to position a Thai brand without losing its identity
- CBAM compliance creating urgency for industrial exporters
- Europe feels bureaucratic and slow compared to SEA

**What they want:** A connector who opens real doors in Helsinki or Stockholm — not another market report

**Conversion trigger:** Warm introduction from mutual contact OR LinkedIn content that names the Thai challenge specifically

**Content that works:** P3 (brand across cultures), P2 (market entry), P4 (partnerships)

**Direction tag:** T→N

**Hashtags:** #Thaibusiness #wellnesseconomy #EUmarket #CBAM #Nordicmarket

## Content Writing Test

Before every piece of content, ask:
> "Which named profile would stop scrolling for this — A or B?"

If the answer is "neither" or "everyone" — rewrite the opener.
