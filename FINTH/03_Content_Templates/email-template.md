# Email Template — FINTH

## Quarterly Corridor Brief Email

Subject line model: "Q[N] [Year] Nordic–SEA corridor brief — 3 things I'm watching"

Body structure:
- Opening: 1 sentence — what this email contains
- Brief summary: 2-3 sentences on the corridor right now
- CTA: link to full brief (PDF or LinkedIn post)
- Sign-off: personal, direct — Jere, FINTH

## Outreach / DM Template

Message types:
1. LinkedIn connection request note (300 characters max)
2. First DM after connection (no pitch, genuine observation)
3. Follow-up DM after they engage with content
4. Re-engagement message (90 days no interaction)
5. Warm brief send note
6. Partner introduction request

Outreach rules:
- Never pitch in the first message
- Reference something specific to the person
- Keep first contact to 3–5 sentences maximum
- Always write as Jere — personal, direct, warm but not familiar
- Thai contacts: warmth before business always

Draft format:
```
Contact type: [Nordic executive / Thai business leader / potential partner]
Message type: [Type 1–6]
Context: [1 line on what you know about this person]
Draft: [the message]
Tone check: [confirm it passes FINTH voice test]
```
