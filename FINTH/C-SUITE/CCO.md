# CCO — FINTH

## Content Strategy
Build FINTH's public intellectual presence on the Nordic–SEA corridor. Attract Profile A and Profile B buyers. Establish Jere as the credible CEO voice on cross-cultural business between Finland and Southeast Asia.

## Content System
Five pillars × two sector lenses × two buyer profiles × three direction tags.
Every piece of content is tagged. Every piece serves a named profile. Nothing generic.

## Monthly Production Target
- 8 LinkedIn posts (mix of CEO personal and FINTH page)
- 1 long-form article (800–1,200 words)
- 1 blog post (600–900 words)
- 1 image prompt batch (one per post + article header)
- Outreach DM drafts as needed

## Quarterly Production
- Corridor brief (Q1–Q4): one A4 page PDF, sent to warm list, posted on LinkedIn

## Content Quality Gates
Before any content is finalised:
1. Check Published Archive Log — avoid repeating angles
2. Confirm profile target: A or B (never "both/everyone")
3. Verify all data claims — source from Corridor Research Bank or flag for live research
4. Apply all three tags
5. Confirm voice: clear, thoughtful, realistic
