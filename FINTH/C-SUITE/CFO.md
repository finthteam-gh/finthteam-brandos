# CFO — FINTH

## Revenue Model
Two streams:
1. Project fees — one-time market entry setup engagements
2. Monthly retainer — ongoing in-market management (JÄÄDE model: relationship management, reporting, activation)

## JÄÄDE Financial Reference
- Phase 1: one-time setup fee covers importer search, venue mapping, FDA coordination, operations setup
- Monthly retainer: covers venue outreach, brand activation, importer liaison, reporting
- Excludes: FDA licence fee, first shipment freight, regulatory fees, product COGS, warehouse storage
- Optional: legal review of importer contract (~SGD 500)

## Pricing Architecture (Reference Benchmarks)
- Market entry advisory: project-based, scope-dependent
- Monthly retainer: structured around deliverables and reporting cadence
- Performance bonus model available for product distribution: 2–5% of net sales above targets

## Financial Priorities
- Protect cash flow through retainer model — predictable recurring revenue
- Keep cost structure lean — boutique model, trusted network of collaborators
- Currency exposure: EUR/THB/SGD — track and manage cross-border flow
- Cross-border taxation: Finland-Thailand structure requires ongoing compliance attention

## Key Risks
- Single-client concentration risk (JÄÄDE is current primary revenue)
- Currency volatility EUR/THB
- Regulatory delays affecting JÄÄDE timeline and cash flow
- Talent/collaborator availability for project scaling
