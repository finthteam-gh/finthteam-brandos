# Colors — FINTH Design System

## Primary Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Warm off-white | `#F5F3EF` | Primary background — all materials |
| Deep forest | `#2E4A3E` | Primary dark — headings, strong elements |
| Warm sand | `#9C7A52` | Accent — highlights, borders, icons |

## Rules

- Never use pure white (`#FFFFFF`) — always warm off-white `#F5F3EF`
- Never use pure black (`#000000`) — always deep forest `#2E4A3E`
- Colour accents: warm sand or deep forest only — no other accent colours
- Background default: warm off-white
- Dark background option: deep forest (for impact sections, covers)

## Image Style

- Background: warm off-white (`#F5F3EF`) or deep forest (`#2E4A3E`)
- Text on image: one line, maximum 8 words, pulled from post hook or title
- Font on image: clean bold sans-serif, aligned left or centre
- FINTH wordmark: small, bottom-right corner, always
- Style: minimal, editorial, quiet — not loud, not corporate, not stock photo
- No people unless documentary — never posed, never stock executive

## Image Dimensions

| Format | Dimensions |
|--------|------------|
| LinkedIn CEO personal posts | 1080 × 1080 px |
| FINTH page posts | 1200 × 627 px |
| Blog header | 1600 × 900 px |

## Image Style by Pillar

| Pillar | Visual direction |
|--------|-----------------|
| P1 Corridor | Abstract map or geographic line graphic — Nordic to SEA route, minimal, navy and warm sand |
| P2 Market entry | Clean workspace or documentary setting, Helsinki or Bangkok, real not staged |
| P3 Brand & culture | Split visual — Nordic geometric restraint left, Thai warm texture right. Abstract, editorial |
| P4 Partnership | Two considered objects in relation — not a handshake. Document, key, connection line |
| P5 CEO worldview | Long-horizon landscape — Nordic coast or Thai bay, desaturated, minimal text |
| Health sector | Clean clinical-warm aesthetic — warm white and sage, subtle craft or wellness visual |
| Green sector | Nordic industrial meets Thai green — wind, leaf, or circuit abstracted |
