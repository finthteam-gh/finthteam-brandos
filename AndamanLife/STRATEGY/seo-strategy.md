# SEO Strategy — Andaman Life

## Core Approach

Authority-first SEO. Every article must be the most useful, accurate answer available for its target query. Quantity follows quality — never the reverse.

## Target Search Intent Categories

**Relocation intent (highest value):**
- "living in Phuket as a foreigner"
- "retiring in Phuket [nationality]"
- "moving to Phuket from [country]"
- "long stay visa Thailand"
- "best areas to live in Phuket"

**Practical intent (high volume):**
- "international schools Phuket"
- "starting a business in Phuket"
- "property for sale Phuket foreigners"
- "leasehold vs freehold Thailand"
- "Thailand Elite Visa review"
- "cost of living Phuket 2026"

**Community intent (loyalty building):**
- "expat groups Phuket"
- "things to do Phuket long stay"
- "Rawai neighbourhood guide"

## Technical SEO Rules (Per Article)

- Focus keyphrase in first 6 words of H1 title
- H1 max 60 characters
- Meta description 140-155 characters — factual, no hype
- First paragraph quotable by AI (structured, direct)
- Keyphrase density 0.5-1% — natural, not forced
- Structured data: tables, lists, bold entities where appropriate
- Internal linking: every article links to at least 2 related AL articles

## Dual Export Protocol

Every article exported in two formats:
1. Notion-ready markdown (internal editorial reference)
2. WordPress-ready HTML (with metadata comment block at top)
