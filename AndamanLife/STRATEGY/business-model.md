# Business Model — Andaman Life

## Revenue Stream Priority

### Tier 1 — Activate First (Low friction, immediate)
**Native / Sponsored Content**
- Local service brands pay for editorial-quality features
- Categories: property agencies, international schools, legal and visa services, healthcare, coworking spaces
- Pricing: [CONFIRM once traffic baseline established]
- Standard: All sponsored content disclosed. Quality threshold applies — only brands Andaman Life would recommend editorially.

**Affiliate Partnerships**
- Revenue share on referrals for high-intent services
- Target affiliate categories: property platforms, health insurance, visa services, international banking, shipping/relocation services
- Pricing: commission per qualified lead or conversion
- Disclosure: affiliate links always disclosed

### Tier 2 — Activate at Traffic Threshold
**Display Advertising**
- Google AdSense or direct ad sales
- Activate when monthly sessions reach [CONFIRM threshold]
- Ad placement: non-intrusive — sidebar, end of article, not between paragraphs

### Tier 3 — Future (Requires Audience Scale)
**Paid Membership / Newsletter Tier**
- Premium resources, curated guides, community access
- Price point: EUR 5-15/month
- Requires: strong free content base and engaged subscriber list first

**Vetted Service Directory**
- Annual listing fee for qualified local service providers
- Categories: legal, property, healthcare, education, coworking
- Revenue: listing fee + featured placement option

---

## Editorial Independence Rule

No revenue stream compromises editorial independence. Sponsored content is always disclosed. Advertisers do not influence non-sponsored editorial coverage. HEARTBAKER would be covered the same way whether or not they were an advertiser.
