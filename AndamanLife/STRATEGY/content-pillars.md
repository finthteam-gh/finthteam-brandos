# Content Pillars and Categories — Andaman Life

## The 10 Categories

| Code | Category | Primary Audience Need |
|------|----------|-----------------------|
| AL-1 | Living in Phuket | Day-to-day life, neighbourhoods, cost of living, utilities |
| AL-2 | Business & Entrepreneurship | Starting a business, BOI, work permits, legal structures |
| AL-3 | Work & Career | Remote work, coworking, finding local employment |
| AL-4 | Lifestyle & Wellness | Gyms, spas, healthcare, mental health, daily routines |
| AL-5 | Food & Dining | Restaurants, markets, food culture for residents (not tourists) |
| AL-6 | Family & Education | International schools, childcare, family life in Phuket |
| AL-7 | Culture & Experiences | Local events, festivals, off-the-beaten-track experiences |
| AL-8 | Guides & Resources | Practical how-to guides — visas, banking, driving, healthcare |
| AL-9 | Property & Housing | Renting, buying, leasehold vs freehold, areas to consider |
| AL-10 | Community & Networking | Expat groups, networking events, building social life |

## Production Priority Order

1. AL-1 — highest search volume, entry point for new audience
2. AL-8 — high utility, strong SEO for specific queries
3. AL-9 — high-intent audience, monetisation potential
4. AL-2 — serves entrepreneur and remote worker reader
5. AL-6 — serves the family relocator
6. AL-3, AL-4, AL-5, AL-7, AL-10 — rotate systematically

## Geographic Zones (Balance Across)

Track coverage across 7 Phuket zones. Maximum 30% from any single zone per month:
- Patong area
- Kata / Karon
- Rawai / Nai Harn
- Chalong / Ao Chalong
- Old Phuket Town
- Laguna / Bang Tao / Cherng Talay
- East coast / Phuket City surrounds
