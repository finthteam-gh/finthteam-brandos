# Audience Persona — Andaman Life

## Primary Reader
European or Nordic, aged 30–60. Living in Phuket long-term or seriously planning to relocate.
Financially independent, retired early, or remotely employed. Chose Phuket for quality of life, not price.

Not: budget backpackers, short-stay tourists, or party seekers.

They read Andaman Life for:
- Accurate visa and residency information
- School options for children
- Business setup and working legally in Thailand
- Property: leasehold, freehold, condo rules
- Healthcare, banking, day-to-day admin
- Community and networking

They expect:
- Respect for their intelligence — no overselling Phuket
- Honest assessment including downsides and complexity
- Specific, current, verifiable information
- European comparison points (not Bangkok, not tourist Thailand)

## Secondary Reader
Nordic 55+ considering Thailand as retirement or long-stay destination.
High disposable income. Reads carefully. Values accuracy over entertainment.
Connects to FINTH's Silver Economy research stream.
