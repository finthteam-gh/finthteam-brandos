# COO — Andaman Life

## Production Rhythm
- Monthly content batch: articles across 4–5 AL categories
- Each article: metadata block → draft → style audit → dual export (Notion + WordPress)
- Publishing: WordPress, scheduled or immediate

## Content Workflow
1. Check published archive — avoid angle overlap
2. Select focus keyphrase — confirm it fits in first 6 words of title
3. Research phase (deep research before outline)
4. Draft in Claude CoWork following AL style standards
5. Style audit against voice-and-tone.md
6. Export: Notion markdown + WordPress HTML
7. Publish and archive

## Partnership Operations
KOL partnerships: profile.md per partner in PARTNERSHIPS/KOLs/[NAME]/
Local brand campaigns: brief.md and deliverables per brand in PARTNERSHIPS/Local_Brands/[NAME]/

## Archive Management
All published articles logged and archived in CONTENT/Blog/published/
Naming: YYYY-MM-DD_AL-[N]_[slug].md
