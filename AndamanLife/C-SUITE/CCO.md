# CCO — Andaman Life

## Editorial Strategy
Every article answers a real question a long-stay resident or serious relocation candidate would ask.
No filler, no SEO stuffing, no tourist content.

## Monthly Batch Target
- 4–6 articles per month minimum
- Span at least 4 different AL categories
- Include at least one deep feature (1,200+ words) per batch
- Include at least one practical guide (AL-8 format) per batch

## Content Quality Standards
- All data attributed or flagged for verification
- Named places, real prices, real businesses
- Honest: acknowledge complexity, include downsides
- Style audit completed before every publish
