# CMO — Andaman Life

## Brand Position
The intelligent resource for long-stay living in Phuket. Monocle-level editorial quality applied to the practical reality of expat life on the Andaman coast.
Not a travel blog. Not a tourist guide. Not an expat forum. A platform that respects the reader.

## Growth Strategy
Primary: SEO — well-structured articles on high-intent search queries
Secondary: Social media — Instagram and Facebook for discovery, LinkedIn for professional expats
Tertiary: Newsletter — direct relationship with loyal readers

## Target Growth Metrics
- Organic search traffic as primary acquisition channel
- Email list building for owned audience
- Monthly content consistency as foundation for all growth

## Partnership Strategy
KOLs: select partners whose audience matches AL reader profile — not lifestyle influencers, but credible voices in expat community
Local brands: vetted, quality-aligned, labelled clearly when commercial

## SEO Priorities
High-intent queries: visa options phuket, international schools phuket, property rules foreigners thailand, retire thailand phuket, remote work phuket
